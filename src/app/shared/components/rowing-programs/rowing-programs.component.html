Novice Crew (Competitive)

The fall program runs August through mid-December. Rowers in their first year of competition start on the Novice squad and should refer to the Novice practice times and race schedule. After a year of racing, rowers move to the varsity squad. Rowers from our program have gone on to row in college and on scholarship, coach high school and college rowing, and have won numerous Youth National Championship titles. Discover your potential this fall! PE credit is available for both varsity and novice teams (read below for AISD PE information). Fall regattas include the Head of the Braozs and Head of the Colorado.

Rowers must register online to secure a place in the program. Rowers need to also fill out a registration packet and bring with you to the first class day.
